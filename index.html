About me

As a child who used to love narrating stories, no wonder, I’ve grown up to be someone who narrates stories but now it’s through data. Often, I remember my early years, and associate it with most of my career choices. I started my first business at the age of ten by utilizing my creativity. Creativity helped me demonstrate my ability of finding insights from business data at a manufacturing firm. Later, as my interest grew in the field of marketing, I took up the challenge of leading a marketing interns’ team at GaoTek Inc. Now, I wanted to combine my love for data and interest in marketing, and that’s how I landed up a marketing analytics internship at a Human Resources start up, where I not only learned about marketing analytics, but devised robust marketing strategies for EU and American clients. The global exposure honed my communication and collaborative skills as I was the point of contact between the CEO and the clients. Recently, I collaborated with a global team of consultants for devising a branding strategy for an EdTech client based out of Texas. My goal in life is to keep my learning curve steep and solving as much as problems. The problem solver in me, took up a project to identify market potential of plant-based meat to mitigate environmental hazards poultry farms possess. Currently, I am working as a data analyst with XN project of Northeastern University in collaboration with Sense, an energy consumption analytics company. Along with this, I'm humbled to have received the opportunity to be one of the founding members of Impact Consulting's Boston Hub. Apart from data and leading projects, I collect coins, and appreciate art in the form of music and painting. I love collaborating with like minded people, hence feel free to reach out to me and see if we can work on something new!
